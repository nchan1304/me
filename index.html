About

Building things that work in the real world.

I'm a product and programme leader with experience across retail technology, SaaS, supply chain, and digital transformation. Over 12+ years I've moved between hands-on delivery and strategic ownership — building teams, managing large budgets, and shipping things that reach real users at scale.

At Avery Dennison I lead the RFID digital transformation programme for one of the largest apparel retailers in the region — managing rollout across 400+ stores across requirements, pilot, full-scale deployment, training, and after-sales support. Alongside this, I work as a product and solution lead in the New Solutions Offering (NSO) capacity, identifying new ways to implement RFID solutions that address market needs and drive adoption across APAC.

At Decathlon India I managed an IT portfolio of ₹55Cr+ (~$6.6M), leading a team of 20+ engineers and 15+ vendors. I launched several India-first products — RFID self-checkout, the country's first smart trolley with POS integration, and a mobile scan-and-go app that reached 5 million users.

At Trinet Zenefits (US HR SaaS) I owned the CX and tooling roadmap — improving key support metrics and contributing $2.5M in measurable impact.

I'm open to senior and leadership roles in Product Management, Programme Management, Delivery, or Digital Transformation — across industries.
